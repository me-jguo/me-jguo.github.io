Hey, I’m Janice!

I’m from a suburban city called Dublin, Ohio, where our token attraction is a field of 109 giant, concrete corn. Now, I’m proud to be at Cal as an EECS major. Outside of CS, I am an enthusiast of music and bad puns. I am happiest when I’m drawing or writing or otherwise creating something new. I am a prolific reader, adherent of the font Calibri, and avid apologist of the Oxford comma.
